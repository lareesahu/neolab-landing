The Problem

5–7 products. None of them fresh.

The modern skincare routine is broken. Men are expected to manage a complex multi-step regimen with products that were formulated months — sometimes years — before reaching their bathroom shelf.

01
Complexity
The average effective skincare routine requires 5–7 separate products. Most men abandon it within weeks. Complexity is the #1 barrier to consistent skincare.
02
Ingredient Degradation
Active ingredients like NMN, peptides, and hyaluronic acid degrade over time. By the time a product reaches the consumer, potency is already compromised — often significantly.
03
No Subscription Intelligence
Existing subscription models are just auto-ship. There is no production timing, no freshness guarantee, and no intelligence about when the customer actually needs their next bottle.

The Solution

One pump.
Nine treatments.

NeolabCare collapses the entire skincare routine into a single, lab-fresh formula. Produced to order. GMP certified. Delivered at peak potency.

9-in-1 Formula Moisturiser · Serum · Eye Cream · SPF · Primer · Post-Shave · Anti-Aging · Brightening · Barrier Repair
Made-to-Order Production No pre-manufactured stock. Every batch is produced for confirmed orders only. Dispatched within 7 days of production.
GMP Certified Facility Manufactured under Good Manufacturing Practice standards. Pharmaceutical-grade quality controls at every stage.
Vacuum Pump Delivery Airless vacuum pump preserves formula integrity. No oxidation. No contamination. Consistent dosing every use.
Subscription Intelligence Delivery cadence is timed around the customer's usage cycle — not a fixed calendar. Next batch is planned before the current one runs out.
