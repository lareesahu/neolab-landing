How It Works
1
Share your referral link
Post your unique link across your platform. Fans who visit get a private access banner and 50% off their first bottle at checkout.
2
They purchase using your code
Your creator code is automatically applied at checkout. Each confirmed order is attributed to you.
3
Commission clears after 7 days
7 days after the customer receives their order (with no refund or dispute), your commission status updates from Pending to Confirmed.
4
Monthly payout
Confirmed commission is paid out monthly to your nominated account. You'll receive a payout summary by email.
