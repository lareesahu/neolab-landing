What is NeolabCare?

NeolabCare is a fresh-made, all-in-one facial cream for men. Produced to order and dispatched within 7 days, it combines nine clinically studied actives — NMN, GHK-Cu, Ergothioneine, Niacinamide, Centella Asiatica, Hyaluronic Acid, Panthenol, Ceramides, and Glabridin — in a single precision vacuum pump bottle.

What does NeolabCare replace?

One bottle replaces your moisturiser, serum, eye cream, post-shave calmer, antioxidant treatment, peptide treatment, barrier repair product, brightening product, and anti-aging cream. Nine products. One pump.

Who is NeolabCare for?

Men who want a simpler, more efficient routine without sacrificing performance. Particularly effective for those who shave regularly, have combination or oily skin, or want anti-aging results without a complicated multi-step regimen.

Why does fresh-made skincare matter?

Most skincare sits in warehouses 12 to 24 months before reaching you. Sensitive actives like NMN and GHK-Cu degrade during that time. NeolabCare produces each bottle to order and dispatches within 7 days — so actives arrive at peak potency, not after a year on a shelf.

What We Left Out

Zero synthetic fragrance. Zero drying alcohol. Zero retinol. Zero AHAs. Every omission was deliberate — each one excluded because it did not align with our philosophy of barrier-safe, high-tolerance skincare.

Tap ? on each card to see what it does

Synthetic Fragrance ?
Added to mask the smell of cheaper base ingredients. Common cause of contact dermatitis and long-term skin sensitisation.
Drying Alcohol ?
Used to create a fast-dry, lightweight feel. Disrupts the skin barrier with repeated use and accelerates transepidermal water loss.
Parabens ?
Cheap, effective preservatives. Linked to endocrine disruption — they mimic oestrogen and accumulate in tissue with long-term use.
Mineral Oil ?
A low-cost occlusive that locks in moisture by sitting on the skin surface. Clogs pores and prevents actives from penetrating.
Retinol
by design ?
Proven anti-aging effect — accelerates cell turnover and stimulates collagen. Causes sun sensitivity, peeling, and requires a separate SPF routine. We achieve the anti-aging outcome through NMN and GHK-Cu without the downtime.
AHAs / BHAs ?
Effective exfoliants that resurface the skin. Increase photosensitivity and require sun protection — limiting when and how often you can use them.
Heavy Metals ?
Trace contaminants found in mass-produced cosmetic ingredients — lead, arsenic, cadmium. Accumulate in tissue over time. We source pharmaceutical-grade actives and batch-test for purity.
Hormone Disruptors ?
A class of compounds — including some UV filters and preservatives — that interfere with hormonal signalling. Absorbed through the skin and detected in blood and urine after topical use.

FAQ

Everything
you need
to know.

If your question isn’t here, email us directly. We answer every message personally.

Can I use it morning and night?+
Yes. The formula is safe for both morning and evening use year-round — no retinol, no AHAs, no sun-sensitising ingredients. The bottle is designed for one pump per day, giving you a 90-day supply. If you prefer to use it twice daily, we recommend choosing a more frequent subscription cadence so your next batch arrives before you run out.
Is it suitable for post-shave use?+
Yes. Post-shave recovery is a core design principle. The formula includes soothing and barrier-supporting ingredients that make it effective immediately after shaving. It absorbs quickly and reduces redness.
How long does one bottle last?+
At one pump per day, a 30ml bottle lasts approximately 90 days. If you prefer to use it morning and night, it will last 45 days.
When will my order ship?+
Your order will be produced after the campaign closes. We dispatch within 7 days of production completion — this is our commitment. Delivery timelines vary by destination: typically 5–10 business days for most of Asia, 10–21 days for international destinations. You will receive your production date and tracking number by email.
Is this suitable for sensitive skin?+
The formula was designed to be gentle — no retinol, no AHAs, no fragrances. However, individual sensitivities vary. We include travel ampoules with every full-bottle order so you can patch-test it first before opening the main bottle.
How does the subscription work?+
Subscribers on Tier 06 and above receive their next batch automatically — every 3 months for Seasonal, every 2 months for Bi-Monthly. We time the production and dispatch so your new bottle arrives before your current one runs out. No reminders, no manual reorders. You can pause or cancel at any time.
What does “produced to order” mean?+
It means we do not manufacture stock in advance. Each batch is produced specifically for the orders received. This eliminates shelf time entirely — your bottle is made for you, then dispatched directly.
