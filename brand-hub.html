02 — Colour System
The Palette.
#0A0A0A
Void Black
Primary background. All digital surfaces. Never use pure #000000.
#141414
Layer
Card and section layer backgrounds.
#1A1A1A
Charcoal
Secondary surface. Hover states.
#C8B89A
Sand Gold
Accents, borders, labels, <em> emphasis. Never as background fill.
#E0D0B0
Gold Light
Lighter accent variant. Hover states on gold elements.
#F5F5F0
Off White
Primary text. Used at 94% opacity as --text.
#C1121F
Crimson
Physical world only. Silk lining, bottle dot, inner label. Never digital.
The Rule of Black
Void Black is a philosophy
All digital surfaces default to #0A0A0A. Never use pure #000000. The warmth of Void Black is intentional.
The Rule of Gold
Sand Gold is a hairline, not a surface
#C8B89A is used for accents, borders, and typographic emphasis only. It is never used as a background fill. Never as a heading or body text colour.
The Rule of Red
Crimson lives in the physical world
#C1121F exists only in the physical world: the silk lining, the bottle dot, the inner label. It is never used in digital contexts. The red is the secret.

04 — Usage Rules
What we never do.
Typography Rule 01
Heading color is always --text
Never apply --accent to heading text. --accent is for labels, borders, and <em> emphasis only. Headings use --text (rgba(245,245,240,.94)).
Typography Rule 02
<em> in headings is weight 400
The serif italic <em> inside .h-hero and .h-section must be font-weight: 400. Not 500. The lighter weight creates contrast with the 500-weight Poppins.
Voice Rule 01
No exclamation marks. Ever.
Not in copy, not in social, not in email subject lines. Short declarative sentences. "One cream. Does it all." — not "One cream that does it all!"
Voice Rule 02
No wellness language
No "ritual", "journey", "self-love", "glow up", "skin barrier era". These are TikTok register. We use clinical language. Trust the customer's intelligence.
Formula Rule 01
9 actives. No Retinol. No Vitamin C.
The formula contains: NMN, GHK-Cu, Ergothioneine, Argireline, Niacinamide, Centella Asiatica, Hyaluronic Acid, Panthenol, Glabridin. Retinol and Vitamin C are in the "What We Left Out" section only.
Formula Rule 02
Registered in the United States
The brand is registered in the United States. Never add Singapore to any geo meta tags, footer copy, structured data, or marketing materials.
