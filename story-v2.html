Why NeoLabCare was created

I wanted something high-performance, easy to stay consistent with, and gentle enough for sensitive skin. I looked for a long time. I could not find it.

The frustration started simply. I wanted a skincare routine that worked at a serious level without asking for a serious amount of time. Something I could use every morning without thinking, and trust that it was doing what it needed to do. Not a ritual. Not a project. Just a product that fit into a real life.

"I kept looking for a product that could do more while asking less."

The more I researched, the clearer the pattern became. Effectiveness almost always meant complexity. Better actives meant more products. More products meant more decisions, more friction, more room for inconsistency. The better the routine, the harder it was to maintain.

I also started to understand something about how the industry works. Products are not formulated for your skin. They are formulated for shelf survival — for the months they spend in warehouses, distribution centres, and retail stockrooms before they reach you. The system is optimized around logistics, not outcomes.

NeoLabCare for Skin  ·  30ml  ·  Produced to Order
"When I could not find one that met that standard,
I decided to build it."

I spent years working inside one of France's most respected clinical skincare brands in China. I learned exactly how products are formulated for retail shelf life. I learned what gets left out, and why. That experience gave me a very clear picture of what a different set of constraints could produce.

NeoLabCare is what I built when I chose a different set of constraints. One premium all-in-one cream. Fresh-made to order. Formulated around what the skin actually needs. Simpler than anything else on the market — not because we cut corners, but because we made different decisions about what mattered.

Maximum dedication. Ultimate simplicity.
Achieving more by doing less.
— Lareesa Hu  ·  Founder, NeoLabCare
